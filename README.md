# 🚀 EduSync AI

### Your Personal AI Learning & Career Mentor

EduSync AI is an AI-powered personalized learning platform designed especially for engineering students.  
It helps students reduce exam pressure, understand concepts deeply, and discover their interests and career paths early.

---

## 🌍 Problem Statement

Engineering students often rely on exam-focused learning, which leads to poor conceptual understanding and weak real-world application skills.  
They also lack early exposure to career paths and discover their interests too late.  
EduSync AI solves this by providing personalized AI-driven learning and career guidance.

---

## 💡 Features

### 🧠 AI Concept Explainer
- Simplifies complex engineering concepts
- Provides step-by-step explanations
- Gives examples, diagrams, and summaries
- Supports conversational learning

### 📄 Smart Syllabus Analyzer
- Upload syllabus TEXT
- AI identifies important topics and weightage
- Highlights difficult and frequently asked concepts
- Generates a smart study roadmap

### ❓ Quiz Generator
- Generates MCQs and concept-based quizzes
- Customizable by topic and difficulty
- Provides instant performance tracking

### 🎯 Interest Discovery
- Analyzes strengths and interests
- Suggests domains like AI, Web Development, Core Engineering, etc.
- Recommends skills, projects, and learning paths

----

## 🔄 Workflow

1. Student opens dashboard 
2. Uploads syllabus TEXT  
3. AI analyzes syllabus  
4. AI explains concepts   
5. Conducts quizzes
6. Track performance 
7. Recommends career paths
